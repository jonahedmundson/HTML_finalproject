Favorite Painting

My favorite painting is not a painting, but an illustration. This illustration is 'White and golden Lizzie stood' by Arthur Rackham. It is the 3rd in a series of 4 illustrations based off of the poem 'Goblin Market' by Christina Rossetti. I like both the illustration and the poem because they deal with the eternally relevant themes of temptation, self-control and forgiveness, and do so in a fairy tale style.
